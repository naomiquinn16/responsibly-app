Problem

The ethics of AI, is a critical challenge for the global tech industry. The speed with which AI is changing the world has urged the industry to incorporate ethical considerations when developing new machine learning products. With almost no laws specifically governing AI, most companies lack a basic AI ethics framework to regulate how their AI is being developed, tested and used.

So this is why we built Responsibly.

Our Vision is to bridge the gap between data science and legal teams so that in the future, they can collaborate and be part of an AI Ecosystem that creates ethical, explainable and interpretable AI products which can be trusted by consumers.

Our Product - Platform as a Service

Responsibly is an AI auditing and reporting platform that helps businesses design, develop and test AI products to ensure they are fair, ethical, explainable & interpretable before being signed off by legal teams and brought to market.

Bias Detection and Removal

Our Bias Detection & Removal tool enables data scientists and engineers by detecting and reducing bias in their data and AI Models.

Legal Compliance Auditing & Reporting

Our Legal Compliance Reporting tool arms legal entities with the information they need to ensure all technological innovation is regulatory compliant.

Explainable AI

Our XAI tool creates transparency between end users and AI by ensuring that the logic behind the outcome of an executed algorithm is explainable.
